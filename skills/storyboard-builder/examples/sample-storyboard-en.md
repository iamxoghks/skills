# Sample Promotional Video Storyboard

Short narrative shooting board

## Framing Guide

![Framing Guide](sample-storyboard_assets/cut-01.png)

- Black outer border: the complete 16:9 landscape frame.
- Blue vertical dashed lines: the central 9:16 safe area retained in a vertical crop.
- Areas outside the dashed lines: visible in landscape but may be removed in a vertical export.
- Keep essential faces, gestures, and props between the blue dashed lines.
